I. Two Assignment

I had one assignment where I should create some web pages,
I had another assignment where I should make a documentary.

So I chose to make a documentary project based on web pages,
to ease my life as I had other assignments.

But can I call it a choice of art if it came as a coincidence?
And can I call it a choice of mine if it was decided in my laziness?

In November 2021, a student at Beijing Film Academy locked himself in a cage and covered his eyes with a mask. A card on its top reads, "Do not leave the cage if you do not have to," a nod to the famous Zero-Covid slogan: "Do not leave the house if you do not have to."
