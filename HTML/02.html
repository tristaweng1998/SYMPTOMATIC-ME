II. Choice of Art

I was born in China, where you don’t need too much self-consciousness to grow up. Everything is mapped out by the starting point you are given, and you just follow the path.

Perhaps it was out of my given resistance to this “givenness” that I pictured myself a future as a filmmaker at a young age, believing that it would open me up to a world I knew but could not feel around me.

Ironically, I did become a filmmaker when I started making documentaries, where I turned my lens to a place I had turned my back on a long time ago.

A poster says, “Doing more Covid tests is the necessary way to freedom.”
