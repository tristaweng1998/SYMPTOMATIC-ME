2. Choice of Art

I was born in China, where you don't need to (or maybe shouldn't) be self-conscious to grow up.
Everything is mapped out by the starting point you are given, and you follow the path.

Perhaps it was out of my given resistance or rebelliousness against this "givenness" that I pictured myself a future as a filmmaker at a young age, believing it would open me up to a world I knew but could not feel around me.

However, it seems ironic that I did become a filmmaker when I started making documentaries, where I turned my camera back to a place I had turned my back to a long time ago.
